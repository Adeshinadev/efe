MEET THE CONVENER

Owajoba Busayo.

Oluwabusayo Owajoba is the CEO of De-Elegant Expertise Palais, a fashion house based in Ado-Ekiti, Ekiti state. Elegant as she is popularly called hails from Emure-Ekiti, Ekiti State. She bagged BSC in Accounting at the prestigious Ekiti state University. In 2017, she launched the First ever fashion event "Ekiti Fashion Exhibition" in Ado-Ekiti, Ekiti state, under her fashion brand ''De Elegant Expertise Palais'' which featured over 200 fashion designers showcased and promoted their designs. This event attracted big brands like ABUAD, Coca-Cola, Lush Hair etc.

OUR VISION

DEEP has a vision to raise a host of innovative fashion creatives with a sense of purpose and fulfilment, and develop individuals to be self-reliant and embrace the ideal of helping others in actualising their dreams.

OUR MISSION

To initiate fashion trends; expand the fashion industry; raise goodwill fashion ambassadors; empower youngsters through fashion seminars and workshops; and promote local fashion brands through fashion show exhibitions in the state.
